About Microgreeney

Growing fresh, clean, and nutritious microgreens for everyday life

We focus on quality indoor farming to deliver fresh microgreens that support healthy eating and better living.

Our Story

Microgreeney started with a simple idea — to make fresh, healthy microgreens accessible to everyone. With a passion for indoor farming, we began growing small batches in controlled environments to ensure consistent quality and hygiene.

Today, we proudly supply microgreens to homes, cafes, and small businesses in Penang, delivering fresh produce grown with care and precision.

Our Mission

To provide clean, nutritious, and affordable microgreens while promoting healthier lifestyles through sustainable indoor farming.
