About Us

Growing fresh microgreens with care and passion.

At Microgreeney, we focus on delivering clean, fresh, and nutrient-rich microgreens grown in a controlled indoor environment.

Our Story

Microgreeney started with a simple goal — to bring high-quality, fresh microgreens to local homes and businesses in Penang. We believe that healthy food should be accessible, clean, and grown with consistency.

With indoor farming techniques, we ensure every batch is grown under controlled conditions, free from outdoor contamination.

What We Focus On

  • Clean indoor growing environment
  • Consistent product quality
  • Fresh harvest for every order
  • Local supply and delivery
